Creators

Cuphead is an indie game released in 2017 by Studio MDHR, created by brothers Chad and Jared Moldenhauer. The game stands out for its unique aesthetic, inspired by 1930s cartoons, with hand-drawn animations and an authentic jazz soundtrack. The story follows Cuphead and Mugman, who, after losing a bet to the devil, embark on a journey to collect the souls of debtors in exchange for their own lives, facing intense and challenging boss battles along the way.

With gameplay focused on difficulty, Cuphead challenges players to overcome complex obstacles and learn from their mistakes, creating a highly rewarding experience. The game has received numerous awards, including the BAFTA for Best Visual Style, and solidified Studio MDHR as one of the leading names in the indie scene. Its success is due to the creators' passion for honoring the history of animation while delivering an immersive and visually stunning challenge.
