Celeste is an indie game released in 2018 by Maddy Makes Games, blending challenging platforming gameplay with an emotionally deep narrative. The game follows Madeline on her journey to climb Mount Celeste, which serves as a metaphor for confronting her internal struggles and mental health issues. The story explores themes such as self-overcoming, depression, and acceptance, providing an experience that goes beyond the physical challenge of the levels, touching the player in a sensitive and realistic way.

With awards such as Best Game at the 2019 BAFTA, Celeste solidified the studio's reputation and gained a dedicated fanbase. Expectations for its future projects remain high, with the promise of continuing to explore deep themes and creating immersive and meaningful experiences for players.

After the success of Celeste, Maddy Makes Games established itself as an innovative studio in the indie scene, creating a unique experience that combines challenging platforming with a deeply emotional narrative about mental health and self-overcoming. The story of Madeline, facing her own demons while climbing Celeste Mountain, resonated with players, while Lena Raine's soundtrack and the pixel art visual design were widely praised.

With Celeste receiving numerous awards and global acclaim, including recognition at the 2019 BAFTA, Maddy Makes Games solidified itself as a studio unafraid to explore deep and challenging themes. The game's fanbase continues to grow, and expectations for the studio's future projects are high, always with the promise of creating experiences that touch players' hearts and make them reflect on personal and universal issues in an authentic and engaging way.
