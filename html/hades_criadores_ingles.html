Creators

Supergiant Games is an independent American studio founded by Amir Rao and Gavin Simon in 2009, with the mission of creating games with a strong authorial identity. The studio gained recognition with the release of Bastion in 2011, but it was with Hades, released in 2020, that it solidified its place as one of the biggest names in the industry. Hades combines roguelike elements with a high-quality cinematic narrative, set in the Greek underworld, where the player takes on the role of Zagreus, the son of Hades, in his attempt to escape. The visual design, with its unique art style and fluid animations, is complemented by the memorable soundtrack by Darren Korb, which enhances the game's immersive experience.

Supergiant stands out for its ability to create rich universes, deep characters, and engaging storylines. With Hades, the studio took this to new heights, winning both critical acclaim and player admiration, and receiving numerous awards, including "Best Action Game" at The Game Awards 2020. The team's dedication to delivering an innovative and detailed experience made Hades a landmark in the action game genre and a modern masterpiece.

After the success of Hades, Supergiant Games solidified its position as one of the most innovative studios in the industry. With a unique combination of roguelike gameplay and an engaging narrative, the game captivated players by offering an intense experience and deep characters, particularly highlighting Zagreus's relationship with the gods of the Greek underworld. The quality of the design and the soundtrack, composed by Darren Korb, was also widely praised.

With Hades winning numerous awards and global recognition, Supergiant continues to demonstrate its commitment to creating immersive and exciting games. The studio maintains a dedicated fanbase and looks ahead with great expectations for its upcoming projects, always striving to surprise and delight with its unique creativity.
